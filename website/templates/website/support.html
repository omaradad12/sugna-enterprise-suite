Support levels

Response targets depend on your subscription and order form. The table below summarizes typical tiers for production environments; your contract may define different hours, regions, or escalation paths for humanitarian deployments.

Standard

Growing teams and single-country programs

  • Business-hours coverage in your agreed region
  • Web and email channels; severity-based prioritization
  • Documented workarounds for non-critical defects

Popular

Priority

Multi-site NGOs with active grant cycles

  • Extended coverage windows and faster initial response
  • Named escalation path for P1 / P2 production issues
  • Coordination with implementation partners where applicable

Enterprise SLA

Large federations and regulated environments

  • Custom response and resolution targets in your agreement
  • Options for dedicated technical account alignment
  • Runbooks for donor audits, payroll peaks, and year-end close

Exact hours, holidays, and languages are defined in your support addendum. Ask your account team if you need 24×7 coverage or in-country escalation for field offices.

FAQ preview

Quick answers for common questions from NGO finance and IT teams. For full documentation, use the knowledge base.

Where is my data hosted, and can we restrict regions?

Hosting geography and residency options depend on your subscription and contractual terms. Your order form or data processing schedule should specify regions and subprocessors; your Sugna account team can confirm the current map for your tenant.

How do backups and disaster recovery work?

We maintain backup and recovery procedures appropriate to a cloud ERP platform. Specific RPO/RPO-style commitments, if any, are stated in your agreement or technical schedule — not on this marketing page alone.

Can Sugna support donor reporting and audit seasons?

Yes. Many customers align cutover, training, and hypercare windows with grant reporting and audit periods. Discuss peak-load support and freeze windows with your implementation lead or customer success contact.

How do I open a ticket if I’m already a customer?

Use the channel identified in your welcome pack or contract — typically your customer portal or a dedicated support mailbox. Including tenant name, module, and impact helps us route severity correctly.
