{# 5. Key features — illustration + copy (Sway: alternating feature rows) #}

Key features

Enterprise foundations built into every deployment.

Multi-tenant

Isolated data per organization with centralized platform operations.

  • Per-tenant domains, branding, and configuration
  • Logical data isolation with encryption at rest
  • Centralized releases, monitoring, and SLAs

Fund accounting

Track restricted and unrestricted funds with clear audit trails.

  • Fund dimension on chart of accounts & transactions
  • Multi-currency with policy-controlled rates
  • Period close, journals, and immutable audit trail

Grant compliance

Align spend and reporting to donor rules and internal policy.

  • Grant budgets, burn rates, and milestone tracking
  • Donor rules, eligibility, and compliance checkpoints
  • Funder-ready schedules and narrative reporting

Role security

Role-based access, approvals, and segregation of duties.

  • Granular roles mapped to finance & program workflows
  • Multi-level approvals and delegated authority
  • SoD policies with sign-off and review trails
