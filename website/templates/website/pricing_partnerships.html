Sugna grows through partners who share our standards for security, ethics, and delivery quality. Explore each track below or start a conversation with our team.

Implementation partners

Systems integrators that lead discovery, configuration, data migration, and organizational change for multi-entity and multi-country clients—supported by playbooks, estimation guides, and escalation to Sugna specialists.

Consulting partners

Advisory firms that align finance, grants, and operations strategy to Sugna’s model—often ahead of a formal implementation or as ongoing optimization and audit readiness.

Technology partners

ISVs and platforms extending analytics, identity, payments, mobility, or vertical depth alongside Sugna’s core modules, with documented APIs and joint roadmap alignment.

Integration partners

Partners who implement supported integration patterns—ETL, iPaaS, field data capture, and donor systems—with test tenants and co-marketing for validated, supportable solutions.

Strategic alliances

Networks, funders, associations, and government programs that want consistent reporting and responsible scale—framework agreements, bulk enablement, and thought leadership on digital transformation in the social sector.

What partners receive

Enablement, deal registration, and repeatable deployment assets so your teams can propose and deliver Sugna with confidence.

  • Solution playbooks and architecture patterns
  • Partner manager coverage and product escalation
  • Joint business planning for sectors and geographies
  • Referral and reseller mechanics where appropriate

Ethics & compliance

Partners uphold data protection, anti-corruption, and honest representation of Sugna capabilities—especially in donor-funded contexts.

  • Security and privacy expectations in shared pursuits
  • Accurate scoping and handover to steady-state support

How we engage

  • Introductory call — fit, geography, sector focus, and capacity.
  • Program alignment — commercial model, training path, and reference expectations.
  • Enablement — technical and functional certification tracks for your leads.
  • First joint opportunity — supported pursuit with clear roles and success metrics.
