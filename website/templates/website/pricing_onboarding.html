Workspace setup

Tenant provisioning, environment strategy (sandbox vs production), branding where applicable, and baseline security settings aligned to your access policy and segregation of duties.

Master data configuration

Chart of accounts, dimensions, programs, grant structures, suppliers, employees, and items—loaded via controlled templates with validation and reconciliation checks before go-live.

Workflow configuration

Approval rules, delegation, posting controls, and exception flows that match your governance—documented for auditors and adapted for HQ vs field operations.

Data migration support

Cutover planning, historical balances, open items, and parallel run support where required. Migration scope is priced separately when it exceeds standard templates.

Role-based training

Curricula for finance, grants, HR, procurement, and operations—delivered in waves so super-users and end users are productive before go-live. Materials can be retained for onboarding new staff.

UAT support

Test scripts, defect triage, and sign-off packs so leadership can certify readiness. Evidence supports donor and statutory audits where applicable.

Go-live & hypercare

Cutover execution, war-room support, and a stabilization window after launch—then transition to steady-state operations and a backlog for phase-two modules.

Delivery phases

A typical engagement follows four phases from blueprint through enablement—tailored in your statement of work.

Phase 1

Discovery & blueprint

Stakeholder interviews, process maps, chart of accounts and dimensions, grant and program structure, segregation-of-duties model, and integration inventory.

Phase 2

Build & configure

Tenant setup, master data templates, workflow and approval rules, role assignments, and connector configuration in line with your security baseline.

Phase 3

Validate & migrate

Iterative UAT cycles, reconciliations, controlled data migration or cutover planning, and documented sign-offs for audit and donor readiness.

Phase 4

Enable & go-live

Role-based training, hypercare support, issue triage, and handover to steady-state operations with a clear backlog for phase-two modules.
