About Sugna Enterprise Suite

We build cloud ERP software for organizations whose work matters — where transparency, compliance, and scale are non-negotiable.

Our mission

Sugna Enterprise Suite helps NGOs, humanitarian agencies, hospitals, and development organizations run complex operations with the same rigor as global enterprises — without losing the agility required in the field.

What we believe

  • Stewardship first — donors and communities deserve clear, timely, and accurate reporting.
  • One source of truth — finance, programs, HR, and supply chain should connect, not fragment across spreadsheets.
  • Security by design — multi-tenant isolation, role-based access, and auditability are core, not add-ons.

Platform approach

Sugna is delivered as a modular suite: adopt Financial & Grant Management first, add Procurement & Logistics, extend to HR, enable AI-assisted auditing, or scale into hospital operations — aligned to your roadmap and funding cycles.
