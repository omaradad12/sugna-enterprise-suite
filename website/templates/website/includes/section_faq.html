Frequently asked questions

Quick answers for IT, finance, and program stakeholders.

Is Sugna multi-tenant and cloud-hosted?
Yes. Each organization runs in a logically isolated tenant with secure cloud delivery and centralized platform operations.
Can we start with finance and grants only?
Absolutely. Modules are adopted in phases — many customers begin with Financial & Grant Management, then add HR, procurement, or hospital workflows.
How do you handle donor compliance reporting?
Grant budgets, restrictions, and funder-ready reporting are built into the platform with audit trails and role-based approvals.
Do you support SSO and enterprise security reviews?
Role-based access, segregation of duties, and documentation for security and compliance reviews are part of enterprise deployments.
